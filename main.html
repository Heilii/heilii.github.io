Villa Ata 1

Set in Fethiye, 11 km from Fethiye Marina and 11 km from Ece Saray Marina, Villa Ata1 offers a seasonal outdoor swimming pool and air conditioning. This villa features a private pool, a garden and free private parking. This villa comes with 6 bedrooms, a kitchen with a microwave and a toaster, a flat-screen TV, a seating area and 3 bathrooms equipped with a hot tub. Towels and bed linen are featured. At the villa guests are welcome to take advantage of a hot tub and a sauna. Villa Ata1 offers a barbecue and a terrace. Butterfly Valley is 15 km from the accommodation, while Kayakoy Ghost Town is 5.1 km away. The nearest airport is Dalaman Airport, 62 km from Villa Ata1.
